Living Things And Their Characteristics

A living thing is a thing that is alive. A living thing is also known as an organism. Living things can be
further classified into the different types of living things:

  • animals
  • plants
  • fungi
  • microorganisms

    • Living Things

  • need air, food and water to survive
  • reproduce to ensure continuity of their own kind
  • grow and develop in size / appearance
  • respond to changes to ensure their own survival

    • Reproduction

  • MAMMALS: Giving birth to young birth alive
  • ALL ANIMALS EXCEPT MAMMALS: Lay eggs
  • FLOWERING PLANTS: Seeds
  • NON FLOWERING PLANTS & FUNGI: Spores
